Ordering FAQ:

  • Q: How do I order?
  • A: Fill out the order form and click Submit. Easy!You will receive an email confirming your order.

  • Q: When do I have to have my order in by?
  • A: Orders will be accepted until 11:59pm on the Wednesday prior to the weekend they will be picked up.

  • Q: Where do I pick up my order?
  • A: All orders can be picked up Friday, Saturday, or Sunday 8am*-6pm at Enberg's Produce, Hager City, WI. Click here for the Enberg's Produce website and directions.

  • Q: How does payment work?
  • A: Payments can be given t the Enberg's Produce staff when picking up an order.

  • Q: Does NJD Bakery deliver?
  • A: We deliver to Enberg's Produce!

  • *Fresh scones are delivered early Friday and Saturday mornings. Come early and nab a few before they disappear!
